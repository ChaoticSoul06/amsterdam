About PawFinds

PawFinds is a community-driven platform designed to help rescue stray, lost, or injured cats and dogs. Our mission is to connect people who spot animals in need with volunteers and rescuers who can provide help.


By allowing users to upload images and share locations of animals, PawFinds helps create a network of compassionate individuals working together to improve animal welfare.


Every report posted on PawFinds increases the chances of an animal receiving timely help and finding a safe home.
